## Challenge Understanding
PromptWars Challenge 4 focuses on utilizing Generative AI to enhance stadium operations and the overall tournament experience for the FIFA World Cup 2026. The judges are looking for real-world impact, operational intelligence, and a cohesive experience—not just a technology showcase.

## Problem Statement Interpretation
Modern sporting venues have abundant data but lack unified operational intelligence. The challenge isn't about building a generic chatbot; it is about building a platform that uses AI to predict, recommend, and coordinate across disparate systems. 

## Assessment Parameter Alignment
- **Innovation**: Applying "Invisible AI" to operational workflows. The UI must expose intelligence, not raw prompts.
- **Feasibility**: The application must look and feel like an official, deployable FIFA operational product.
- **Impact**: Demonstrating clear value for multiple personas (Fans, Volunteers, Operations, Security).
- **Design Excellence**: Utilizing a premium design language (inspired by Apple, Stripe, Linear, Airbnb) rather than developer tooling aesthetics.

## Judge Psychology
Judges evaluate based on outcomes and narrative. They are drawn to products that solve human problems elegantly. 
- They care about coordinated venue operations, access management, safety, and procedures.
- They **do not** care about rendering technology, polygon counts, or Three.js implementation details.
- We must sell the *intelligence*, not the *code*.

## Differentiation Strategy
Most submissions will build reactive AI chatbots or spatial visualizations. We differentiate by building a proactive **Stadium Intelligence Platform**.
- **No Chatbots as Primary UI**: AI operates as the reasoning engine behind the scenes.
- **Premium Feel**: Our UI will be indistinguishable from a top-tier Silicon Valley product.

## Persona Strategy
Showcase the platform's versatility through four connected personas:
1. **Fan**: Emphasize inclusive, stress-free journeys.
2. **Volunteer**: Highlight intelligent task dispatch and translation.
3. **Operations**: Showcase coordination and predictive flow.
4. **Security**: Demonstrate incident prevention and rapid resolution.

## Scoring Strategy

## Repository Strategy

## Deployment Strategy

- Supabase for backend stability.
- Ensure zero downtime or performance hiccups during the judging period.

## Demo Strategy
